Tags

The tags are colour coded depending on whether they are mandatory and must be applied to all entries or whether they are optional with a default value being used if they are not present. New tags are bold.

Text

TagDefaultPurpose
DescriptionReference to the text that containms a description of an instance of this esionage mission. The text may be defined in any of the files in \Assets\XML\Text
TechPrereqNO_TECHThe tech that a player needs to know in order to conduct this espionage mission
TypeThe internal reference to this espionage mission

Integer

TagDefaultPurpose
iAttitudeModifier0If true, the mission will change the attitude of the target towards the spys civ
iBuyCityCostFactor0Percentage change to the cost of a buy city mission, depending on the gamespeed and base mission cost. If successful, the mission will convert the city to the players team.
iBuyTechCostFactor0Percentage change to the mission cost based on how much research went into the technology.
iBuyUnitCostFactor0Percentage change to the mission cost based on the production cost of the unit. If successful, the mission will convert the unit to the players team
iCityInsertCultureAmountFactor0Percentage change to the city culture that is converted to yours. (A value of 5 will convert 5% of the city culture)
iCityInsertCultureCostFactor0Percentage change to the mission cost based on the existing culture in the city.
iCityPoisonWaterCounter0The number of turns that the cities water supply will be poisoned with this mission.
iCityRevoltCounter0The number of turns that the city will be in revolt with this mission.
iCityUnhappinessCounter0The number of turns of unrest that the city will be subjected to with this mission.
iCost0The Base Espionage Point cost the mission will require
iCounterespionageMod0Percentage change to the cost of espionage missions carried out by the victim harder. It lasts for the number of counter espionage turns.
iCounterespionageNumTurns0The number of turns that the counter espionage modifier applies.
iDestroyBuildingCostFactor0Percentage change to the mission cost depending on the production cost of the building. If successful, the mission will destroy the building
iDestroyProductionCostFactor0Percentage change to the mission cost depending on the number of hammers already spent. If successful, the mission will wipe out the existing production
iDestroyProjectCostFactor0Percentage change to the mission cost depending on the production cost of the project. If successful, the mission will destroy the project
iDestroyUnitCostFactor0Percentage change to the mission cost depending on the production cost of the unit. If successful, the mission will wipe out all progress on training the unit
iDifficultyMod0Percentage change to the difficulty of the mission
iPlayerAnarchyCounter0The number of turns the target nation will be in anarchy
iRemoveCorporationsCostFactor0The modifier to the base cost of a mission in order to remove a corporation from the city
iRemoveReligionsCostFactor0The modifier to the base cost of a mission in order to remove a religion from the city
iSabatogeResearchCostFactor0The modifier to the base cost of a mission in order to sabotage the targets current research
iStealTreasuryTypes0Percentage of the mission targets gold that is stolen
iSwitchCivicCostFactor0Percentage change to the cost of the mission based on how much the target likes the civic
iSwitchReligionCostFactor0Percentage change to the cost of the mission based on how much the target likes the religion
iVisibilityLevel0Used to turn on city visibility (Only 0 or 1 will have any effect. I don't know why Firaxis didn't make this Boolean.)
iWarWearinessCounter0The amount the war weariness in the target city is increased, this is modified by ganme speed and other factors

Boolean

TagDefaultPurpose
bDestroyImprovement0If true, the mission destroys the improvement that the spy is on.
bDisablePower0If true, the mission will disable power in a city
bInvestigateCity0If true, allows the unit to investigate an enemy city
bIsPassive0If true the mission does not need to be actively selected by the player and is always active, such as viewing enemy cities
bIsTwoPhases0If true, the mission requires a secondary screen to determine the exact mission, e.g. sabotaging a building requires a secondary screen to select the building.
bNoActiveMissions0If true, the mission requires no other active missions to be taking place
bNuke0If true, the mission will be considered to have used a nuclear device
bSeeDemographics0If true, allows the player to see the statistics of the target civ
bSeeResearch0If true, allows the player to see the research target of a rival
bSelectPlot0If true, requires a unit to be on a game tile. (Examining foreign countries research is considered a espionage mission and does not require a tile)
bTargetsCity0If true, the mission can only be carried out when the spy is in a city
