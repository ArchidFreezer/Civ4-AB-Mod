Tags

The tags are colour coded depending on whether they are mandatory and must be applied to all entries or whether they are optional with a default value being used if they are not present. New tags are bold.

Text

TagDefaultPurpose
DescriptionA reference to the text files, which contain an entry for this GameSpeed
HelpAdditional help text that will be displayed in the Platypedia
TypeThe internal name of this GameSpeed

iCulturePercent0This value is not read by the dll so has no purpose
iFeatureProductionPercent0Percentage change in the rate which features spread to neighbouring tiles

iGreatPeoplePercent0Percentage change in the threshold for creating great people
iGrowthPercent0Percentage change in the growth threshold of cites
iHurryConscriptAngerPercent0Percentage change in the time taken for the anger from whipping to subside
iHurryPercent0Percentage change in the hammers gained from hurrying a building or unit
iImprovementPercent0Percentage change in the time taken for an improvement to upgrade
iInflationOffset0Percentage change in the number of turns before inflation begins
iInflationPercent0Percentage change in the inflation rate
iResearchPercent0Percentage change in the cost of researching a tech
iTrainPercent0Percentage change in the production cost of training a unit
iUnitDiscoverPercent0Percentage change in the amount of research a great person can provide
iUnitGreatWorkPercent0Percentage change in the amount of culture a great person can provide
iUnitHurryPercent0Percentage change in the amount of hammers a great person can provide
iUnitTradePercent0Percentage change in the amount of gold a great person can provide from a trade mission
iVictoryDelayPercent0Percentage change in the time taken for a spaceship to reach Alpha Centuria
